About This Game



TITLE : The Last of Us™ Part I
GENRE : Action, Adventure
DEVELOPER : Naughty Dog LLC
PUBLISHER : Naughty Dog LLC
Rating : 18+
RELEASE DATE : 3 Mar, 2023

Experience the emotional storytelling and unforgettable characters in The Last of Us, winner of over 200 Game of the Year awards. In a ravaged civilisation, where infected and hardened survivors run rampant, Joel, a weary protagonist, is hired to smuggle 14-year-old Ellie out of a military quarantine zone. However, what starts as a small job soon transforms into a brutal cross-country journey.
