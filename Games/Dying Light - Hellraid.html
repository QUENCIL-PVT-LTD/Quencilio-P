About This Game

TITLE : Dying Light Hellraid
GENRE : Action
DEVELOPER : Techland
PUBLISHER : Techland
AGE : 18+
RELEASE DATE : 26 Jan, 2015

Hellraid is the fourth and latest Downloadable Content expansion pack for Dying Light released on August 13th, 2020. It is a DLC representing the same named game Hellraid from Techland that ceased development in the second quarter of 2015. The DLC puts an old Hellraid console in The Tower, which when triggered opens a portal to Hell and enters the Hellraid world. It was welcomed poorly due to lack of content, where only the Raid mode was enabled at first.
