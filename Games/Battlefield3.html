About This Game

TITLE : Battlefield 3™
GENRE : Action
DEVELOPER : DICE
PUBLISHER : Electronic Arts
AGE : 18+
RELEASE DATE : 28 Oct, 2011

Battlefield 3 features the combined arms battles across single-player, co-operative and multiplayer modes. It reintroduces several elements absent from the Bad Company games,the prone position and 64-player battles on PC.the ground area is limited for Xbox 360 and PS3, though fly space remains the same. The game features maps set in Paris, Tehran, Sulaymaniyah in Iraq, New York City, Wake Island, Oman, Kuwait City.
