Feature

Discover the breath-taking sights
From thick jungles to snow-capped mountains, explore every inch of stunningly beautiful environments in spectacular detail.Enjoy a host of enhanced graphical adjustment features such as adjustable Texture and Model Quality, Shadows, Reflections and Ambient Occlusion.
Feel the thrilling gameplay in your hands
Experience haptic feedback and dynamic trigger effects designed for UNCHARTED: Legacy of Thieves Collection by playing with the DualSense™ controller through a wired connection to your PC. With full control remapping and support for the DualShock®4 controller.
Optimize the adventure
Unearth a wide variety of modes and PC-centric enhancements such as improved UI for game options, reimagined user interfaces, the addition of scale sliders for some menus, GPU and VRAM detection and management options, as well as adding Auto Pause.
