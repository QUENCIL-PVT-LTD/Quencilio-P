About This Game

TITLE : Lunistice
GENRE : Action, Adventure, Indie
DEVELOPER : A Grumpy Fox
PUBLISHER : Deck13
AGE : 18+
RELEASE DATE : 10 Nov, 2022

Lunistice is a linear 3D platformer that is heavily inspired by the 32-bit era of yesteryear. Follow Hana the Tanuki on her adventure through her dreams! Explore the different dreamscapes to guide her to her final destination - The Moon - and try to uncover the secrets of "The Lunistice" along the way! Explore a multitude of different dreamscapes created from Hana's memories! Jump and run through an old shrine, a fancy water park or a world made out of food!
